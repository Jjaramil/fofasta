Our history and Beginnings.

  • 2006

    An unexpected Beginning

    On June 12, 2006, the now Pbro. Merleau Nsimba Ngoma sent an email to Fasta requesting their incorporation to our institution. After almost four years of fluid communications, in May 2010, Merleau made his first trip to Argentina to know the work. What caught his attention the most it was the work “of humanization and Christianization with young people ”and“ the way Fasta knows how to link the culture with the message of Christ and elevate man in culture and in faith. " On his return to Africa, he was entrusted with the foundation of a ruca in Kinshasa.

  • 2010

    The adventure begins

    In September 2010 ruca Bakanja was founded, in honor to Isidoro Bakanja, beatified by Pope St. John Paul II to whom the Church of Congo has as patron saint of the Young Catholics With the ruca and a large youth group began the presence of Fasta in Africa.

  • 2011

    Strong foundations

    In the parish San Antonio de Padua de Kinshasa, about 120 men and women gathered to hopefully build a Christian community that, From the charism of Fasta, be a contribution to the training of young people in your country. In May they made the first march under the name "Saint Thomas of Aquino", and ascended Mount Mangengenge.

  • 2012

    A new seminarian

    In November 2012 and on the occasion of the 50th anniversary of Fasta, Merleau traveled again to Argentina to participate in the central celebrations and to the same time joined as a seminarian of the Apostolic Priestly Fraternity Thomas Aquinas.

  • 2014

    New needs

    In January 2014, the then seminarian Merleau Nsimba Ngoma, returned to his country to visit the communities Fasta dades in Africa. In his return, he enunciated what it was what they needed, both physical as spiritually said communities Especially he referred to the need for that Congolese fasteans will visit Argentina in order to know and live the style of Fasta and communicate it faithfully in your country; get a stable place for the ruca and sustain some activities of young people who have difficulties for the socio-economic situation.

  • 2015

    Help appeared

    In 2015, the Fasteans joined the solidarity campaign that the entire City Militia, through the apostolic community Pope Francis, urged for sustain the work in that continent. Minzoto, which in African language can work dubbed as "the dreams of Africa", is the name they chose to explain their Desires to have your own space. "There's a lot material and economic needs in the mission of the movement. You have to establish structures minimums, such as a chapel, education of catechists, residence for the priests of the work, but also to sustain the charities, of education and human development, ”said seminarian Merleau Nsimba Ngoma in a report presented early

  • 2015

    More help

    In November, Mag. Dan Olofsson and his wife, Christine Benedikta de Olofsson, recognized for his outstanding every humanitarian action in various places in Africa were invited by the authorities of Fasta and via- They went to Argentina. That visit had as an objective that marriage know the Movement and also present the action plan apostolic of our institution in Kinshasa (Minzoto Project).

  • 2016

    Presence starts

    In February 2016, it was incorporated The Fasteans were taken to the Seminary of the Priestly Fraternity Congolese Dieumerci Matondo Ngoma and Reneidi Kayembe Kayemba. In March, the Pbro. Eduardo Lloveras made a new visit to the city of Kinshasa in order to continue with the community and institutional accompaniment with the fasteanos of the place.

  • 2017

    The labor continues

    In January 2017, Daniel Medina Basla was appointed as Promoter for the Fasta Kinshasa Foundation, with the main mission of promoting, from his condition of the layman of the work, that in that foundational community the message of Christ, the Catholic faith is lived and the foundation of the movement and the accomplishment of its works are sought, by working in coordination with the Promotion Directorate Apostolic and with the Head of the Fasta Kinshasa Foundation. Will also be the person in charge of advising and accompanying the successive stages of construction of the project and remodeling to be carried out in the building of the institutional headquarters, in order that it is adapted to the purposes, guidelines and criteria established in due course by the Presidency of Fasta.

Isidoro Bakanja School

First of many planned schools

Isidoro Bakanja is thought to be a school that offers integral education at an accesible price for families in Kinshasa. This project is committed in giving quality education to improves kids oportunities in the future, so that they can be protagonists of their own country's history. At Fofasta we believe this is the best and the hardest way to help people in the Democratic Republic of Congo

  • Date: June 2019
  • City: Kinshasa
  • Ambit: Culture

Cultural center for the young

The cultural center's main objective is to give tools and resources for young people that are curious and want to develop their own capacities. This cultural center is tought to be a place where the ypung can find a cultural exchange with people in the same reality as theirs and people all over the world thanks to the connectivity planned for the place.
